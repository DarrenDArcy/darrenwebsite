What Is Social Engineering?

Scammers and hackers use human psychology to implement their attacks using the 6 spheres of influence. Social engineering is a type of security attack where scammers fraudulently attempt to trick their victims into performing a task or divulging sensitive information.



Social engineers and "hackers" have the same goals and are often the same but instead of hacking networks social engineers "hack" people instead.
