Quid Pro-Quo



Quid pro quo involves manipulating a person into divulging confidential information. Say for instance you receive a call from IT services asking you to divulge your private or confidential information as they are upgrading to a new system which requires all staff to give their username and password , IT services would never do this so this is a clear example of quid pro quo.
