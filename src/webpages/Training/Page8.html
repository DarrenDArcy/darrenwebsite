SMS-phishing



The above text message is a clear attempt at phishing . Again like the email at a quick glance it may look legitimate, however any real look will show its flaws. There is a lot of things wrong with the email that clearly indicate it is illegitimate points this out. Firstly does it pass the "smell test" are you a part of a best buy message list? also what are the odds that you will be randomly selected to receive a thousand dollars?. Again like the email the poor spelling "besy buy" is a clear giveaway.

Another clue that this is a phishing email is the spelling errors. A company like LinkedIn wouldn't have spelling errors like "accaunt".
